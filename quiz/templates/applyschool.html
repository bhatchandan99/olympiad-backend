What's so special about International Olympiads?
With so many Olympiad exams around, you should read the following before you offer these exams to your students
Indeed a unique Olympiad Exam!

  1. International School Olympiads is a new Olympiad foundation

  2. These assessments take place when the school curriculum is complete.

  3. We conduct both, online and pen/paper mode of examination. The online examination is completely proctored through webcams. There's a mock test for you to get the exposure of giving an online exam.

  4. There would be 3 Practice Tests in the form of downloadable pdf to understand the format of exam.

Fee Structure and Account Details

For classes 2 to 11, the total fee is Rs. 225 (Rs 150 - Registration fee and Rs 75 - cost of 3 Practice Tests) for every subject per student ($10 for International students). While for class 1, the total fee is Rs. 175 (Rs 100 - Registration fee and Rs 75 - cost of 3 Practice Tests) for every subject (English, Mathematics and Science) per student ($8 for International students). There should be minimum 50 students from a school to participate in Unicus Olympiads.
