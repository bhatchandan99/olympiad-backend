Critical Thinking

The International School Logical Reasoning Olympiad (ISLRO) is based on forming judgements and testing Logical Reasoning skills.Classes 1-12 can take part in this exam comprising three mocks and one final paper. Students of class 1 to 4 will get minutes to complete the exam and students of class 5 to 12 will get a total of minutes. The exam will follow the pattern of multiple choice questions. Students taking part in the logical reasoning olympiad will receive a comprehensive report of their performances to help them assess their strengths and weaknesses.
